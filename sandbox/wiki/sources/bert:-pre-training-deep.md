**Authors:** Jacob Devlin, Ming-Wei Chang, Kenton Lee, Kristina Toutanova (2018) **arXiv:** 1810.04805 This paper introduces a new language representation model called BERT, which stands for Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers. Unlike recent language representation models such as ELMo and the OpenAI GPT, BERT is designed to pre-train deep bidirectional representations from unlabeled text by jointly conditioning on both left and right context in all layers.
