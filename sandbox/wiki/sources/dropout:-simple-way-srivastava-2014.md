Year: 2014 Journal: Journal of Machine Learning Research 15 (2014) 1929-1958 Deep neural nets with a large number of parameters are very powerful machine learning systems. However, overfitting is a serious problem in such networks. Large networks are also slow to use, making it difficult to deal with overfitting by combining the predictions of many different large neural nets at test time.
